As online retail continues to expand globally, understanding the right growth metrics is essential for e-commerce platforms to scale effectively. These indicators help businesses optimize performance, customer experience, and profitability.

Key Growth Metrics

  • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Total cost of acquiring a new customer
  • Average Order Value (AOV): Revenue per transaction
  • Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): Total projected revenue from a customer over time
  • Conversion Rate: Percentage of visitors who complete a purchase
  • Cart Abandonment Rate: Percentage of users who leave without completing checkout
